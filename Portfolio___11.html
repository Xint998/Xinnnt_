陷落星河   (Ipad Paperless Notebook Design)

Exhibition

 

The advent of the Apple Pencil has significantly boosted creativity and productivity. For art enthusiasts and students, it provides a highly sensitive and precise tool that makes drawing, sketching, and note-taking much easier. After its release in China, many students started using it with the iPad Pro for note-taking and creating beautiful, paperless journals. This inspired me to identify a small business opportunity. During my free time at university, I created a template for a paperless notebook and sold it as a virtual product on Taobao. It is compatible with popular iPad note-taking apps like Notability and Notebook, helping students organize their study notes efficiently. This not only saves paper but also frees up space in their bags and reduces weight. Through this experience, I designed and created product displays and detail pages for my Taobao store, which sparked my interest in web design.
