Irises by the Pond

8.5 * 11 inch

 

Appreciating Claude Monet's artwork "Irises by the Pond" at the Virginia Museum of Fine Arts in a Critical Inquiry class. After conducting research, I wrote some personal commentary on the painting, analyzed Monet's Impressionist style, and shared my understanding of Monet's significance to the Renaissance. Based on the artwork itself, I designed some patterns and integrated the text and these images to create a handmade book about the piece "Irises by the Pond." The cover of the book was inspired by the hazy beauty of light and shadow that is so important in Monet's works.
