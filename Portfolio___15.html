Plant Zine

8.5 * 11 inches

The project focuses on creating a zine book that reflects one's current state. I chose to create illustrations using the silhouettes of decorative plants in my home. Personally, I believe that plants in a home serve not only a decorative purpose but also create a unique atmosphere of self-cultivation and tranquility. When people see or smell plants, it can alleviate a serious and stressful environment. Therefore, I aim to convey a sense of simplicity and calm to the audience.
