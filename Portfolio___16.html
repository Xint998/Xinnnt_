Children's Hospital Wayfinding System Design Exhibition

Exhibition

 

The project aims to design a wayfinding system for a children's hospital. Team members: Ying Ying, Xinyu Na, and myself. Since we want the hospital to provide children with a sense of fairy tale adventure rather than fear, we decided, after group discussion, to use the theme of "Alice in Wonderland." We will use the four suits of playing cards as markers to guide the children.
