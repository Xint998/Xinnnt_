MEMORY FO(U)R SEASONS

Website

 

A collaborative project featuring a website dedicated to collecting memories of seasons, including poetry about the seasons and favorite movies during each season. My assigned task was winter. Winter has always held a special place in my heart. I remember waking up to the world blanketed in a thick layer of snow, the air crisp and cold against my cheeks. So I created eight winter memory posters predominantly themed in blue.
