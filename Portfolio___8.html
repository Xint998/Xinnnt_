IKEA Research Mapping Website

Website

 

In my Core III course, I conducted an extensive investigation into various aspects of the Ikea brand, including its history, style, and product design philosophy. I delved into a comprehensive study of the Ikea brand, learning how it evolved from a small-scale furniture store into a globally recognized household name. I also analyzed Ikea's design style, highlighting its distinctive Swedish influence and the characteristics of minimalist modernism. Furthermore, I explored Ikea's product design philosophy, emphasizing core principles of sustainability, functionality, and affordability. This course provided me with a deeper understanding of Ikea's recipe for success and how to apply these principles to future innovation and design endeavors.
