When designing the visual effects for my DJ friend's logo, I was deeply inspired by the energy and mystery in his music. This logo not only symbolizes his unique style but also reflects his passion and innovation in music. We discussed his feelings and inspirations behind his music, and I ultimately chose to incorporate cyberpunk elements into the design. The futuristic tech vibes combined with American-style typography perfectly captured the electronic elements and spirit of exploration in his music, making the logo a distinctive symbol of his brand.
