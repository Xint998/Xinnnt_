Ipad Paperless Notebook Design

Exhibition

 

As an extension of the MEMORY FO(U)R SEASONS website project, we allocated four spaces for the exhibition, each representing one of the four seasons. For each space, we designed calendars spanning three months and invited visitors to record their memories of the day on sticky notes specifically designed for the assigned season. These sticky notes were then placed on the corresponding dates. To facilitate this process, we created virtual indoor renderings of the exhibition spaces.
